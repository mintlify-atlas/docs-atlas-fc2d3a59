## Prayer tracking

### Track daily prayers

### Prayer statuses

Each prayer can have one of several statuses:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Prayed">
    Prayer completed on time, with optional jamaah indicator
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pending">
    Prayer time has arrived but not yet marked as prayed
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Late">
    Prayer performed after its time expired
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Missed">
    Prayer not performed, needs qada (makeup)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Qada">
    Makeup prayer for previously missed prayer
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Qada (makeup) prayers

Manage prayers that need to be made up:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automatic qada list">
    Missed prayers automatically added to qada list with:
    - Original date
    - Prayer name
    - Days pending
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record makeup prayers">
    Mark qada prayers as completed with:
    - Date completed
    - Link to original missed prayer
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track progress">
    Monitor:
    - Total qada prayers pending
    - Recently completed makeups
    - Oldest pending makeup
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Qada prayers are sorted by date to help you make up the oldest missed prayers first.
</Note>

## Fasting tracker

### Track fasting days

Record different types of fasts:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ramadan fasts" icon="moon">
    Track obligatory Ramadan fasting with:
    - Complete Ramadan calendar
    - Daily completion status
    - Exemption reasons
  </Card>
  <Card title="Voluntary fasts" icon="star">
    Record Sunnah and voluntary fasts:
    - Mondays and Thursdays
    - White days (13, 14, 15)
    - Arafah, Ashura, Shawwal
  </Card>
  <Card title="Makeup fasts" icon="rotate-left">
    Track Ramadan makeup fasts:
    - Link to original missed day
    - Completion date
    - Pending count
  </Card>
  <Card title="Other fasts" icon="calendar">
    - Expiation (kaffarah) fasts
    - Vow (nadhr) fasts
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Fasting statuses

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Fasted">
    Successfully completed fast for the day
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Not fasted">
    Day passed without fasting (for voluntary fasts)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Exempted">
    Exemption from fasting with reason:
    - Travel
    - Illness
    - Menstruation
    - Pregnancy
    - Breastfeeding
    - Elderly
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Makeup due">
    Missed Ramadan fast that requires makeup
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
