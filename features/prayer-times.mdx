## Asr calculation methods

Choose between two schools of jurisprudence for Asr prayer timing:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Standard (Shafi'i)">
    Used by Shafi'i, Maliki, and Hanbali schools. Shadow length = object length.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Hanafi">
    Used by Hanafi school. Shadow length = 2 × object length.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## High latitude adjustments

For regions with extreme latitudes where twilight persists, Nimaz offers three adjustment methods:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Middle of the night">
    Calculates Fajr and Isha based on the middle point between sunset and sunrise
  </Step>
  <Step title="Seventh of the night">
    Uses 1/7th of the night duration for twilight calculation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Twilight angle">
    Adjusts the angle used for twilight calculations
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prayer time notifications

### Notification types

Nimaz provides two types of notifications to keep you informed:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Prayer time notifications">
    Alerts you when it's time for each prayer. Includes:
    - Prayer name and time
    - Islamic reminder or hadith
    - Quick action to mark prayer as prayed
    - Option to play Adhan
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pre-prayer reminders">
    Advance notice before prayer time arrives. Configure reminder time from 5 to 60 minutes before each prayer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Configuring notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable notifications">
    Grant notification permission when prompted during onboarding or in app settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select prayers">
    Choose which prayers should trigger notifications (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set pre-reminders">
    Optionally enable pre-prayer reminders and set the advance notice time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Adhan sound">
    Select from multiple Adhan recitations or use silent notifications
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Sunrise is shown for reference but doesn't trigger notifications by default, as it's not a prayer time.
</Note>

## Time adjustments

Fine-tune prayer times to match your local mosque or personal preference:

- Adjust each prayer time by ±30 minutes
- Adjustments are saved per location
- Useful for syncing with local masjid schedules

<Info>
  Time adjustments are applied after the calculation method computes the base prayer times.
</Info>
