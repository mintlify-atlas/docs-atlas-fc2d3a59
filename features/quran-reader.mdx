The Quran reader in Nimaz provides a comprehensive reading experience with multiple viewing modes, translations in various languages, audio recitations, and powerful bookmark management.

## Reading modes

Choose between two distinct reading experiences:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Ayah-by-ayah mode">
    Read verses in a scrollable list format with:
    - Arabic text with Uthmani script
    - Optional Tajweed highlighting
    - Translation below each verse
    - Transliteration (romanization)
    - Ayah-level bookmarks and notes
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mushaf (page) mode">
    Traditional Mushaf page layout with:
    - Authentic page-by-page Quran text
    - Madani Mushaf page numbers (604 pages)
    - Swipe left/right to turn pages
    - Page-level reading progress
    - True-to-print Mushaf experience
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Navigation options

Access any part of the Quran quickly:

<Steps>
  <Step title="By Surah">
    Browse all 114 Surahs with:
    - Arabic and English names
    - Transliteration
    - Revelation type (Meccan/Medinan)
    - Number of verses
    - Quick Surah info and themes
  </Step>
  <Step title="By Juz">
    Navigate by 30 Juz divisions for:
    - Daily reading schedules
    - Memorization plans
    - Tracking monthly progress
  </Step>
  <Step title="By Page">
    Jump directly to any Mushaf page number (1-604)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resume reading">
    Automatically returns to your last read position
  </Step>
</Steps>
